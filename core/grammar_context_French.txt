LANGUAGE & STYLE:
Target language is French. Use formal register for UI (vous), flexible for lore.
Game terminology should use natural French phrasing — avoid anglicisms.
Keep gender consistency across related terms.

TERMS:
Common French words: le, la, les, un, une, des, et, pas, avec, pour, est, sont, dans, sur, que, qui, par, plus, mais, aussi, comme, tout, tous, toute, toutes, bien, très, faire, être, avoir, pouvoir, devoir

GRAMMAR:
French nouns have gender (le/la) and number. Adjectives agree in gender and number.
Definite articles contract: à + le = au, de + le = du.
Partitive articles (du, de la, des) for mass nouns.
Negation: ne ... pas surrounds the verb.
Accents are mandatory: é, è, ê, ë, à, â, î, ï, ô, ù, û, ç.

PLACEHOLDER PROTECTION:
Never translate placeholders like {0}, %s, %d, $variable.
Keep all __SHLD_N__ tokens exactly as-is.
