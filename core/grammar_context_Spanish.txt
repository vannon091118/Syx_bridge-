LANGUAGE & STYLE:
Target language is Spanish. Use formal "usted" for UI commands, tú for casual lore.
Game terminology should sound natural — not calques from English.

TERMS:
Common Spanish words: el, la, los, las, un, una, y, no, con, para, es, son, en, de, que, por, más, pero, como, todo, muy, también, hay, ser, estar, tener, hacer, poder, ir

GRAMMAR:
Spanish nouns have gender (el/la) and number. Adjectives agree in gender and number.
Two verbs for "to be": ser (permanent) vs estar (temporary).
Personal "a" before animate direct objects.
Inverted question marks (¿) and exclamation marks (¡) are mandatory at sentence start.
Accents are mandatory: á, é, í, ó, ú, ü, ñ.

PLACEHOLDER PROTECTION:

Keep all __SHLD_N__ tokens exactly as-is.
