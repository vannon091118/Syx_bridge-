LANGUAGE & STYLE:
Target language is Italian. Use formal "Lei" for UI, natural for lore.
Italian prefers melodic flow — avoid harsh consonant clusters.

TERMS:
Common Italian words: il, la, i, le, un, una, e, non, con, per, che, di, da, in, su, è, sono, ha, hanno, più, come, anche, tutto, molto, bene, fare, essere, avere, potere, volere

GRAMMAR:
Italian nouns have gender (il/la) and number. Adjectives agree in gender and number.
Definite articles combine with prepositions: di+il=del, a+il=al, da+il=dal, in+il=nel, su+il=sul.
Subject pronouns are often omitted (pro-drop).
Accents are mandatory: à, è, é, ì, ò, ù. Grave accent for most; acute for é only.

PLACEHOLDER PROTECTION:
Never translate placeholders like {0}, %s, %d, $variable.
Keep all __SHLD_N__ tokens exactly as-is.
