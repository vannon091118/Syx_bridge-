LANGUAGE & STYLE:
Target language is Chinese (Simplified, zh-CN). Use natural Chinese phrasing.
Keep translations concise — Chinese is more compact than English.
Game names should use established Chinese translations if they exist in the community.

TERMS:
N/A — Chinese does not have common function words in the same sense as European languages.

GRAMMAR:
Chinese uses Hanzi characters (CJK Unified Ideographs: U+4E00–U+9FFF).
No spaces between words — word boundaries are contextual.
No grammatical gender, number, or case — meaning comes from word order and particles.
Use 了 for completed actions, 的 for possession/description, 们 for plural (people only).
Tone marks are optional in game text (use plain characters for compatibility).
Full-width punctuation: ， 。 ！ ？ 「」 『』

PLACEHOLDER PROTECTION:
Never translate placeholders like {0}, %s, %d, $variable.
Keep all __SHLD_N__ tokens exactly as-is.
